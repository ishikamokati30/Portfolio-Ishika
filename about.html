🌱 Campus Involvement & Leadership

Being an active part of various student organizations has played a significant role in shaping my personality beyond academics. These experiences have strengthened my leadership skills, creativity, teamwork, and sense of social responsibility.

  • REEF – Shri Ramdeobaba College Engineers for Environment Forum
    Social Media & Literary Team Member

    REEF works with a mission towards building a sustainable and environmentally conscious community. Through activities like morning nature trails and awareness sessions on animals and birds, the club promotes ecological responsibility. As a member of the Social Media and Literary sector, I contributed to content creation, awareness posts, and written material to support the club’s initiatives.

  • NSS – National Service Scheme
    Co-Head, House of Laws

    Under NSS, I served as the Co-Head of “House of Laws”, where I played a key role in organizing and managing events centered around debates and group discussions. This experience enhanced my leadership, coordination, and communication skills while encouraging active participation in social awareness and civic discussions.

  • RRC – Red Ribbon Club
    Publicity Head

    As the Publicity Head, I was responsible for promoting events and initiatives focused on health awareness and social causes. I handled creative publicity strategies, digital promotions, and engagement activities to ensure wider participation and visibility for the club’s campaigns.

  • CompuSys – CSE Committee
    Creatives Head

    CompuSys is the official Computer Science & Engineering committee of our college, organizing technical and non-technical events for CSE students. As the Creatives Head, I led the design and creative direction for posters, social media content, and event branding—blending technical culture with strong visual storytelling.
